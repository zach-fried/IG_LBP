✴️Full Body Mobility Routine for Beginners ✴️
🚨this is easy to follow mobility routine for beginners or for anyone who is too stiff 🙆‍♀️ 
.
In this video you’ll hit major joints(neck, thoracic spine, lumbar spine), shoulders, elbows, wrists, hips, knees, ankles and feet.

😊This entire routine should take less than 20 minutes to complete.
Try it when you wake up or before bed😉
Stay tuned for more😊
—
1️⃣ 90/90 Switches
2️⃣ Full Spine Cat/Camel
3️⃣ Quadruped to Down Dog
4️⃣ Quadruped Shoulder CARs
5️⃣ Sidelying Thoracic Open Books
6️⃣ Supine Hamstring to Groin
7️⃣ Wrist / Elbow Flexion & Extensions
8️⃣ Alternating Foot / Ankle Plantarflexion & Dorsiflexion
